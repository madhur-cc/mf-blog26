FAQ

Can I make the payment directly using upi?

Yes. Please transfer to sehatvan@oksbi and let us know the transaction id. This way you also save around 3% transaction fees.

Can I see some pictures of the place?

Sure. Check out Life at Sehatvan post.

What if I wish to postpone my booking?

You can! You can make one free postponement within next 3 months from your booking date.

What if I wish to cancel my booking?

We understand. Unpredictable things happen. We all prepare, but sometimes plan don't come to fruition. If you cancel before arrival, we will refund 50% of your amount. If you cancel after arrival or after postponement we won't be able to refund your amount.

Can my *family and children come in this?

Yes! Your children are welcome to this retreat. Infants upto 5 years don't have to pay anything. Kids from 5 years to 12 years will have to contribute 50% (INR 7000).

Everyone above 12 years contributes the normal amount.

Is it safe to come as a woman on my own?

Absolutely! Listen to this participant's experience - click here.

Is this suitable for homemakers?

Homemakers used to be conventionally housewives whose routines were interlinked with providing for their family. Today people of all gender are taking responsibility of being a homemaker. This retreat is specially designed keeping in mind lifestyle needs and responsibilities of Homemakers. By working on their own health they can have a significant impact on the health of the whole family.
