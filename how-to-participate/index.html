Modes of Participation

We welcome those who are interested in Sehatvan's vision of building endurance, happiness and longevity.

You can participate with us in five different modes:

  1. Online : Educate yourself about health, longevity and happiness through Sehatvan's online resources - Video Series and Articles.
  2. Home Module : Get one-on-one guidance at home to progress on your health goals.
  3. Personal Forest Modules: Join one of the ten personalized modules at Sehatvan Campus (Bhopal, India) with choice of your dates.
  4. Study Camps: take part in focused camps organised at fixed dates. Participation is subsidized by Friends of Sehatvan.
  5. Internship : 6-week blended (online + campus) internship for learners.

You will be unable to participate with Sehatvan in the following situations:

  • You require critical medical attention and hospital facilities.
  • For any acute or infectious diseases.
  • You are unable to take care of your daily needs and require an attendant.
  • Lack of self-motivation to participate
